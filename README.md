# Enterprise Credit Risk & Loan Portfolio Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The Enterprise Credit Risk & Loan Portfolio Intelligence Platform is an end-to-end banking analytics solution designed to evaluate loan portfolio performance, monitor credit risk exposure, and support data-driven lending decisions.

This project combines SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI to analyze customer lending behavior, assess portfolio quality, identify high-risk segments, and provide executive-level insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

Financial institutions manage large lending portfolios across multiple customer segments and products. Monitoring portfolio quality, identifying high-risk borrowers, and minimizing default exposure are critical for maintaining profitability and regulatory compliance.

The objective of this project is to:

- Assess credit risk exposure across loan portfolios
- Monitor portfolio performance and delinquency trends
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Evaluate lending concentration risks
- Support strategic credit decision-making through analytics

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze loan portfolio performance across customer segments
- Monitor key credit risk indicators and portfolio quality metrics
- Identify potential default risk patterns
- Evaluate product and regional lending performance
- Build executive dashboards for portfolio monitoring
- Generate actionable insights for risk mitigation

## Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of simulated retail banking lending data including:

- Customer Information
- Loan Products
- Loan Amounts
- Credit Scores
- Interest Rates
- Repayment History
- Delinquency Indicators
- Default Status
- Geographic Segmentation

### Dataset Scale

- 100,000+ Customer Loan Records
- ₹850M+ Loan Exposure Evaluated
- Multiple Lending Products
- Multi-Region Portfolio Coverage

## Key Achievements

- Analyzed 100,000+ customer loan records to evaluate portfolio risk exposure and lending performance.

- Assessed ₹850M+ loan exposure across customer segments, products, and regions.

- Developed 20+ SQL queries to identify delinquency trends, default patterns, and high-risk borrowers.

- Built interactive Power BI dashboards tracking default rates, portfolio utilization, and credit risk KPIs.

- Identified high-risk customer segments contributing disproportionately to portfolio losses.

- Delivered actionable insights supporting risk mitigation and portfolio optimization decisions.
